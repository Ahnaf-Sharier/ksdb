About Us

Established in 1985, Bright Future College has been a cornerstone of quality education in the region. We offer a wide range of programs designed to meet the evolving needs of students and the job market.

We offer undergraduate and graduate programs in Science, Arts, Commerce, and Professional studies. Our curriculum is designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Notice Board

Notice #1 June 15, 2023

Mid-term examinations will commence from next Monday. All students are requested to check the examination schedule on the college website and prepare accordingly.

Notice #2 June 10, 2023

The college will remain closed on June 20th for a local holiday. All academic activities will resume as usual from the following day.

Notice #3 June 5, 2023

Registration for the annual sports competition is now open. Interested students can register their names with the sports department by June 25th.

Notice #4 May 28, 2023

The library will have extended hours during the examination period. From June 18th to July 5th, the library will remain open until 9:00 PM.

Notice #5 May 20, 2023

Career counseling session for final year students will be held on June 30th. All interested students are requested to register with the placement cell.

Notice #6 May 15, 2023

The college canteen will remain closed for renovation from May 25th to June 5th. Alternative arrangements have been made for food services.

Notice #7 May 10, 2023

Scholarship applications for the academic year 2023-24 are now open. Eligible students can apply through the college portal by May 30th.

Notice #8 May 5, 2023

The annual cultural fest will be held from July 10th to 12th. Students interested in participating can register with the cultural committee.

Notice #9 April 28, 2023

Computer lab maintenance scheduled for this Sunday. The lab will be unavailable from 8 AM to 2 PM. Plan your work accordingly.

Notice #10 April 25, 2023

Guest lecture by renowned scientist Dr. Robert Langdon on "Future of Artificial Intelligence" on June 15th at 2 PM in the main auditorium.

Notice #11 April 20, 2023

Last date for submission of project reports for final year students is June 30th. No extensions will be granted beyond this date.

Notice #12 April 15, 2023

Inter-college debate competition registration starts from next week. Interested students should contact the English department.

Notice #13 April 10, 2023

College bus routes and timings have been revised. New schedule is available at the administration office and college website.

Notice #14 April 5, 2023

Workshop on "Effective Study Techniques" will be conducted on May 20th. Registration is free but mandatory due to limited seats.
